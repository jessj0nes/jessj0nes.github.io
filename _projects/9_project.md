Development of a prototype mobile application for the **Skin Cancer Research Fund** (June 2022), awarded 80%.

- Provides the means for a patient to **track the development of moles** over time and share with their clinician.
- **3D body-model** and mole positioning system to pinpoint mole location accurately.
- Assisted photography guides and image ghosting to aid image repeatability.
