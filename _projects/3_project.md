BSc thesis supervised by **Dr. Tilo Burghardt** (May 2023), presenting a novel approach to 3D corallite reconstruction from micro-CT scans of coral colonies. Awarded the **Hargrave Prize** for best undergraduate thesis.

- Deep-learning pipeline employing a **UNet-ViT architecture**, accepting volumetric inputs to generate segmentation volumes of corallite regions.
- Creation of a **novel topological-based loss function** to support domain-specific segmentation and consistent segmentation geometry.
- Fully automated system using the **Blender API** to convert segmentation volumes into interactive 3D object data for structural analysis of individual corallites.
