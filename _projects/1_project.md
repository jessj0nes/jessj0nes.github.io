This work, published at **AAMAS 2026**, assesses the zero-shot affordance inference capabilities of **Vision-Language Models (VLMs)** specifically for non-humanoid robots.

Key contributions:

- Demonstrates the limitations and capabilities of large foundation models to infer affordances in the context of non-humanoid robotics.
- Identifies an inherent **conservative bias** in model inference — an important observation for systems design and operational safety.
